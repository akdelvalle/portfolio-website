A little bit about me!

I'm a UI Designer passionate about minimal and clean design. I believe in making products both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
Working in pediatric healthcare prior to pursuing design is where I learned to appreciate the importance of listening intently, empathizing, and providing patients with the best possible care. This experience gave me the skills to susccessfully manage user experiences.
When I'm not designing or creating you'll find me running, relaxing on the beach reading a good book, or traveling.
I am currently located in Pembroke Pines, Florida and looking for full-time remote work but I am open to discussing any opportunity. Let's create together!
